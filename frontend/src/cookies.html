Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-20

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience, and provide usage insights.

2. How We Use Cookies

We categorize cookies as follows:

  • Essential: Required for core functionality (session handling, security, form interactions). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics (optional): Help us understand aggregate usage patterns (e.g., which features are used most). These are only enabled if you opt in.

3. Cookies We Set

  • cookiePrefs – Stores your consent choices (essential + analytics flags).
  • bp_username – Stores your chosen username to keep you logged in (functional/essential).
  • bp_last_activity – Timestamp of last interaction to support automatic logout for inactivity (security/essential).

4. Managing Your Preferences

You can adjust your cookie settings anytime by clicking the Cookie Settings link in the app header or clearing your browser storage. Changing your choices updates the cookiePrefs entry immediately.

5. Third-Party Tools

No third-party analytics or marketing tools are currently loaded. If we add optional analytics (e.g., privacy-focused metrics), they will only run if you have enabled Analytics cookies.

6. Data Retention

Cookie preference data remains until you clear it or we change the storage schema. Session-related values may reset automatically after inactivity.

7. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or regulation. Material changes will be highlighted in the changelog below.
