This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies in our website and apps (the “Services”). It complements our Privacy Policy and is designed to comply with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They can be essential (necessary for the site to function) or non‑essential (e.g., analytics or advertising). We also use local storage and session storage for similar purposes.

2. Our current setup

We only use essential cookies and local storage at this time. That means:

  • No analytics, advertising, or social media tracking cookies are used.
  • Essential cookies and storage are used for security, session management, and your preferences.
  • Because only essential cookies are used, consent via a cookie banner is not required under PECR. If this changes, we will request your consent first.

  • Cloudflare may set strictly‑necessary cookies when providing security (e.g., bot management, DDoS protection) and CDN functionality to deliver our site.

5. Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that essential cookies are required for the Service to function correctly, and disabling them may break some features.

6. Future changes

If we introduce non‑essential cookies (for analytics or similar), we will display a consent banner and provide a preferences centre to manage your choices. This page will be updated accordingly.
